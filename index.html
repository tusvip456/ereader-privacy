EReader - Data Deletion Instructions

We respect your privacy. If you wish to request the deletion of your personal data associated with your Facebook account from the EReaderOpt application, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to tu1lehongphong@gmail.com.
  2. In your email, please provide the following information:
    • Email subject: "Facebook Data Deletion Request"
    • Your Facebook user ID (you can find this in your Facebook account settings).
    • (Optional) Any additional information that may help us identify your account.
  3. We will process your request and delete the relevant data within 30 business days. We will send a confirmation email once the data has been deleted.

If you have any questions, please contact us via email: tu1lehongphong@gmail.com
